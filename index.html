Pet Loss Grief Support

Grieving a pet can be deeply painful. These resources may help you find professional or peer support during this time.

  • ASPCA Pet Loss Hotline (USA): Check the ASPCA website for current hours.
  • Veterinary School Support Lines: Many university veterinary programs offer pet loss hotlines. Search your local area for updated numbers.
  • Crisis Support: If you are in immediate distress or at risk of self‑harm, contact your local emergency number or a national crisis hotline (such as 988 in the U.S.).

Information here is provided for convenience only. Always confirm hotline availability and details from official websites before relying on a number or service.

This site does not provide medical, psychological, or veterinary advice. If you are struggling, please reach out to a licensed professional or crisis service.

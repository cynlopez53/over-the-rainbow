Pet Loss Grief Support

Grieving a pet can be extremely painful. These resources may help you find professional or peer support.

  • ASPCA Pet Loss Hotline (USA): 877‑474‑3310 (check ASPCA site for current hours).
  • University Veterinary School Pet Loss Support Lines: Many veterinary schools run pet loss hotlines; search your local area for updated numbers.
  • Crisis Support: If you are in immediate distress or at risk of self‑harm, contact your local emergency number or a national crisis hotline (for example, 988 in the U.S.).

Information here is provided for convenience only. Always confirm hotline availability and details from official websites before relying on a number or service.

This site does not provide medical, psychological, or veterinary advice. If you are struggling, please reach out to a licensed professional or crisis service.
